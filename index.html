ABOUT

hello, world


I'm Luca, a Milan-based student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engeneering, at the Polytechnic School of Milan.

I am a curious, creative and deeply passionate human, whose goal is to work as a game designer.

Since 2019, I've been a team member of Dynamis PRC, the Formula Students team of my university, as a software developer for the Driverless Department. In particular I was in charge of the safety of the autonomous system of the car.

In 2021, I also founded Pixelmug, a student-only game development team of which I am currenty the team leader. This project started as a way to move our first steps in the world of game development which is why we are currently developing our first game prototype.

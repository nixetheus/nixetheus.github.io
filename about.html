ABOUT

I'm Luca, a Milan-based student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Engineering of Computer Systems, at the Polytechnic University of Milan.

I am a curious, creative and deeply passionate human, whose goal is to become a game designer and create breathtaking new experiences.

Since 2019, I've been a team member of Dynamis PRC, the Formula Students team of my university, as a software developer for the Driverless Department. In particular I was in charge of the safety of the autonomous system of the car.

In 2021, I also founded Pixelmug, a student-only game development team of which I am currenty the team leader. This project started as a way to move our first steps in the world of game development. We are currently developing our first game prototype.
